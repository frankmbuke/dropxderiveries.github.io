Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about DropX Delivery

All orders must be placed through our mobile app. Download the DropX Delivery app from the App Store or Google Play Store, create an account, and start ordering from restaurants, groceries, or DropX Store farm produce.

We serve all areas of Lilongwe including City Center, Area 3, 9, 10, 12, Area 43, 47, 49, Likuni, Mtandile, Kawale, and Chinsapo. If you're unsure about your area, please check in our mobile app or contact us.

DropX Store is our own farm produce marketplace. We work directly with local farmers to bring you the freshest vegetables, fruits, and farm products. All DropX Store items are available for delivery through our app.

We're planning to expand to Mzuzu in Q1 2024, Blantyre in Q2 2024, and Zomba in Q3 2024. Follow us on social media or check our app for updates on expansion plans.
