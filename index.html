Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about DropX Delivery

You can place orders through our mobile app, website, or WhatsApp. Download our app for the best experience, or visit our website and click "Order Now". For WhatsApp orders, message us at +265 123 456 789.

We currently serve all major cities in Malawi including Lilongwe, Blantyre, Mzuzu, and Zomba. We're rapidly expanding to more towns. Check our "Areas We Serve" section for the complete list and to request service in your area.

Delivery times vary based on location and service type. In-city deliveries typically take 30-60 minutes for food and groceries. Package delivery within the same city is 1-2 hours, while inter-city deliveries take 3-6 hours depending on distance.

We accept multiple payment methods including cash on delivery, mobile money (Airtel Money and TNM Mpamba), and bank transfers. Some restaurants and stores also accept card payments through our app.

You can track your delivery in real-time through our website or mobile app. Simply enter your tracking number in the tracking section, or check your order status in your account. You'll receive SMS updates throughout the delivery process.
