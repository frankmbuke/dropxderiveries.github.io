Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

How we protect and handle your personal information in Malawi

Our Commitment to Privacy

At DropX Delivery, we take your privacy seriously. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services in Malawi.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you use DropX Delivery, we may collect:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth
  • Payment Information: Airtel Money details, TNM Mpamba details, billing address
  • Delivery Information: Delivery addresses, delivery instructions, recipient names
  • Identity Verification: Government ID (for delivery partners), driver's license
  • Communications: Your conversations with customer support

1.2 Usage Information

  • Order History: Restaurants you order from, items purchased, delivery locations
  • App Usage: How you interact with our app, features used, time spent
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Location Data: Real-time location for delivery tracking (with your permission)

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Restaurant and store partners about your orders
  • Payment processors for transaction verification
  • Delivery partners for service completion

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide Services: Process orders, arrange deliveries, send notifications
  • Improve Our Services: Analyze usage patterns, optimize delivery routes, enhance user experience
  • Communicate With You: Send order updates, respond to inquiries, share promotions
  • Ensure Security: Prevent fraud, verify identities, protect against unauthorized transactions
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with Malawian laws and regulations
  • Customer Support: Resolve issues, handle complaints, process refunds

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only as necessary:

3.1 With Service Providers

  • Restaurants and Stores: Order details for preparation
  • Delivery Partners: Pickup and delivery locations, customer contact for delivery coordination
  • Payment Processors: Transaction details for payment processing
  • Customer Support Tools: Communication platforms to assist you

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by:

  • Malawi courts or law enforcement
  • Regulatory authorities
  • Legal proceedings

3.3 Business Transfers

In case of merger, acquisition, or sale, your information may be transferred to the new entity.

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted using SSL/TLS encryption
  • Secure Storage: Data stored in secure, access-controlled servers
  • Access Controls: Strict employee access policies and regular audits
  • Payment Security: PCI-compliant payment processing
  • Regular Monitoring: 24/7 monitoring for suspicious activity

While we take every precaution, no online transmission is 100% secure. We strive to protect your information but cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

Under Malawian law, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Objection: Object to certain data processing

To exercise these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@dropxdelivery.mw

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in to your account
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze how you use our services
  • Personalize your experience

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect functionality.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be stored and processed on servers located outside Malawi. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this policy.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information:

  • As long as your account is active
  • To comply with legal obligations (typically 5 years for transaction records)
  • To resolve disputes and enforce agreements

After account closure, we may retain anonymized data for analytics.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be notified through:

  • Email to registered users
  • In-app notifications
  • Notice on our website

Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or concerns:

12. Complaints

If you believe we have violated your privacy rights, you have the right to lodge a complaint with:

  • DropX Delivery's Data Protection Officer (first point of contact)
  • Malawi Communications Regulatory Authority (MACRA)
  • Malawi Human Rights Commission

We will respond to all complaints within 30 days.

Your Consent

By using DropX Delivery services, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.
